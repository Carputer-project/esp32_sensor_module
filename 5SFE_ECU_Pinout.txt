Toyota 5S-FE ECU Pinout
=======================
Terminals of Engine ECU

Connects to: ESP32 Sensor Module (192.168.4.20, UDP 5001)
Voltage dividers: 5.6K+10K (0.641x) on all ECU signal taps

CONNECTOR E01 (Top Row)
------------------------
E01     - POWER GROUND
E02     - POWER GROUND
No.10   - INJECTOR
No.20   - INJECTOR
STA     - STARTER SWITCH
IGT     - IGNITER
E1      - ENGINE GROUND
NSW     - NEUTRAL START SWITCH
EGR     - EGR VSV
ISCC    - ISC VALVE
ISCO    - ISC VALVE
OX1     - OXYGEN SENSOR

CONNECTOR E02 (Bottom Row)
---------------------------
G⇔     - DISTRIBUTOR
E21     - SENSOR GROUND
G1      - DISTRIBUTOR
NE      - DISTRIBUTOR
IGF     - IGNITER                              → ESP32 GPIO 26 (digital interrupt, RISING)
T       - CHECK CONNECTOR
IDL     - THROTTLE POSITION
THA     - AIR TEMP. SENSOR
VC      - VACUUM SENSOR / THROTTLE POSITION SENSOR
PIM     - VACUUM SENSOR (MAP)                  → ESP32 GPIO 32 (ADC1_CH4, 5.6K+10K divider)
PSW     - THROTTLE POSITION SENSOR
THW     - WATER TEMP. SENSOR (ECT)             → ESP32 GPIO 33 (ADC1_CH5, 5.6K+10K divider)
E2      - SENSOR GROUND

RIGHT SIDE TERMINALS
---------------------
ACA     - A/C AMPLIFIER
OSFC    - OVER SPEED FUEL CUT TERMINAL
B/K     - STOP LIGHT SWITCH
ACT     - A/C AMPLIFIER
ELS     - HEADLIGHT and DEFOGGER
SPD     - SPEED SENSOR                         → ESP32 GPIO 25 (digital interrupt, FALLING)
FC      - CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY
A/C     - A/C MAGNET SWITCH
VF      - CHECK CONNECTOR
BATT    - BATTERY
W       - WARNING LIGHT
+B1     - MAIN RELAY
+B      - MAIN RELAY

SENSOR / SIGNAL SUMMARY
------------------------
E01, E02, E1    - Power / Engine ground
E2, E21         - Sensor ground
No.10, No.20    - Injectors
STA             - Starter switch
IGT, IGF        - Igniter          → IGF on GPIO 26 (RPM sensing)
NSW             - Neutral start switch
EGR             - EGR VSV
ISCC, ISCO      - ISC valve
OX1             - Oxygen sensor
G⇔, G1, NE     - Distributor (crank angle / RPM)
T, VF           - Check connector
IDL, PSW        - Throttle position sensor
THA             - Air temperature sensor
VC              - Vacuum sensor / Throttle position sensor supply
PIM             - Vacuum sensor (MAP)           → GPIO 32
THW             - Water temperature sensor (ECT) → GPIO 33
ACA, ACT        - A/C amplifier
OSFC            - Over speed fuel cut
B/K             - Stop light switch
ELS             - Headlight and defogger
SPD             - Speed sensor                   → GPIO 25
FC              - Circuit opening relay
A/C             - A/C magnet switch
BATT            - Battery
W               - Warning light
+B, +B1         - Main relay supply voltage

--- ESP32 Pin Assignments (Sensor Module) ---

ESP32 PIN   ECU SIGNAL    TYPE              NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GPIO 32     PIM (MAP)     ADC1_CH4 (analog) 5.6K+10K divider, 0-5V→0-3.3V
GPIO 33     THW (ECT)     ADC1_CH5 (analog) 5.6K+10K divider, 0-5V→0-3.3V
GPIO 34     VTA (TPS)     ADC1_CH6 (analog) 5.6K+10K divider, 0-5V→0-3.3V
GPIO 25     SPD           Digital in (int)  5.6K+10K divider, FALLING edge
GPIO 26     IGf (RPM)     Digital in (int)  5.6K+10K divider, RISING edge
GPIO 13     Driver door   Digital in pullup GND-switch + 1K series + 10K pullup
GPIO 14     Passenger dr  Digital in pullup GND-switch + 1K series + 10K pullup
GPIO 27     Trunk         Digital in pullup GND-switch + 1K series + 10K pullup
GPIO 21     Hood          Digital in pullup GND-switch + 1K series + 10K pullup
GPIO 16     (free)        UART2 RX          Freed up for GPS / fingerprint / etc.
