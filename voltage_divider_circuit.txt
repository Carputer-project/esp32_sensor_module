FUEL LEVEL & OIL PRESSURE SENDERS (120О© pullup to 3.3V)
--------------------------------------------------------
  The factory sender provides a variable resistance to GND (chassis ground).
  We feed 3.3V through a 120О© resistor to the sender wire, forming a
  voltage divider. The ADC reads the voltage at the junction.
  This does NOT disturb the factory dash gauge.

  в•җв•җв•җ FUEL LEVEL в•җв•җв•җ

  Sender: ~3О© (full)  вҶ’  ~110О© (empty)

  ESP32 3.3V в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ 120О© в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”¬в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ GPIO 35 (ADC1_CH7)
                           в”Ӯ
                   fuel sender terminal (variable R to chassis GND)

  в•җв•җв•җ OIL PRESSURE в•җв•җв•җ

  Sender: ~3О© (0 psi)  вҶ’  ~100О© (100 psi)

  ESP32 3.3V в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ 120О© в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”¬в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ GPIO 36 / VP (ADC1_CH0)
                           в”Ӯ
                  oil pressure sender terminal (variable R to chassis GND)

  в•җв•җв•җ VOLTAGE TABLE в•җв•җв•җ

  в•җв•җв•җ NOTES в•җв•җв•җ
  - GPIO 39 (VN) unused: wire a 10K resistor to GND to stop ADC crosstalk
  - Sender body/shell = chassis GND = ESP32 GND (common ground)
  - 120О© value chosen for best voltage spread across the expected range


DOOR SENSORS (GND-switch, direct to ESP32)
----------------------------------------------
  The door switch connects directly to GND when open.
  No dome light circuit involved вҖ” clean 0/3.3V logic.

  в•җв•җв•җ WIRING в•җв•җв•җ

  Door switch в”Җв”Җ 10KО© (series, 1/4W) в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ GPIO (INPUT_PULLUP)

  Door OPEN  = switch closed to GND вҶ’ GPIO = LOW
  Door CLOSED = switch open вҶ’ GPIO pulled HIGH by internal pullup

  The 10K is protection-only (ESD/wrong-wire). ESP32 internal pullup
  (~45K to 3.3V) handles the pullup вҖ” no external pullup needed.

  в•җв•җв•җ PER DOOR в•җв•җв•җ

  GPIO 13 в”Җв”Җ 10K в”Җв”Җ Driver door switch  (INPUT_PULLUP)
  GPIO 14 в”Җв”Җ 10K в”Җв”Җ Passenger door switch (INPUT_PULLUP)
  GPIO 27 в”Җв”Җ 10K в”Җв”Җ Trunk switch         (INPUT_PULLUP)
  GPIO 21 в”Җв”Җ 10K в”Җв”Җ Hood switch          (INPUT_PULLUP)


POWER
------
  ECU +B (12V) в”Җв”Җв–ә DC-DC converter (set to 3.3V) в”Җв”Җв–ә ESP32 VIN
  ECU E2 (GND) в”Җв”Җв–ә ESP32 GND (common ground)

  NOTE: Do NOT load the ECU's internal 5V regulator.
        Use a dedicated DC-DC converter from 12V.

TEST PROCEDURE
---------------
  1. With key ON, engine OFF, measure at ECU pin:
     - PIM: ~3.5-4.5V (atmospheric)
     - THW: ~2.0-2.5V (cold engine ~68В°F)
     - VTA: ~0.5V (throttle closed)

  2. Measure AFTER divider at ESP32 GPIO:
      - Should be ~0.643 Г— ECU pin voltage (10K+18K divider)
      - T/VF: idle ~4.5-5V вҶ’ ESP32 GPIO 19 ~2.9-3.21V

  3. ESP32 serial output should show:
     - coolant: ~180-220В°F when warm
     - rpm: idle ~700-900
     - map: idle ~15-25%, key-on ~100%
     - throttle: closed ~0%, WOT ~100%
